MoodSWING

"An innovative digital platform to help with the problem of indecisiveness"

Here at The MoodSWING Group, we feel that the problem of indecisiveness has reached an all time high. People are unaware of what to do with there day and have no basis for their decsion making process. With the innovative mood based technology, MoodSWING will provide users with personalized suggestions based on their emotional state throughout the day.
